Beacon

Beacon is an open technical standard for discovery of genetic variants and their annotations. Beacon is developed by the Global Alliance for Genomics & Health (GA4GH).

COVID-19 Beacon

The COVID-19 Beacon is a service that enables discovery of genetic variants that have been identified in genomes of the COVID-19 virus and associated information (e.g. location).

Searching the COVID-19 Beacon

To search the COVID-19 Beacon, enter information about the variant of interest (genome position, reference and alternate allele) and click the search button. Information about variants that are aggregated by this service will be listed. This service may not aggregate all available datasets.

Partnering

This service is intended to help share data on COVID-19 as broadly as possible, connecting a global ecosystem of data generators and researchers. For assistance on making genome sequence data available through this service or using it for your analyses, please contact us using the information below.
